Safe Spaces

Create secure, invite-only spaces perfect for friends, family, and close circles. Think of confidential spaces for corporations, but for the family and everyone! Only the group has access to your shared moments, keeping them away from the public eye.

Organize Your Connections

BAPU lets you organize your social life into distinct types of spaces to fit your needs:

💰 Rooms

Your space for monetization. Create exclusive content, offer premium subscriptions, and earn directly from your most dedicated supporters.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Bubbles

Your private space for family. A secure and confidential environment to share daily updates, photos, and moments with your closest relatives.

🤝 Circles

Your space for friends. Hang out, share interests, and stay connected with your social circles in a safe and engaging environment.
