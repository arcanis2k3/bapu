Early Access — Free Handle Program

We are currently offering **free custom Bluesky handles** under selected domains we operate. This program exists to **test, validate, and harden our identity infrastructure**.

Early users help us stress-test automation, DNS control, DID resolution and security flows. In return, you receive **a free professional handle**.

What You Get

  • Custom handle under our domains (e.g. @yourname.bapu.app or @yourname.idroot.org )
  • Fully managed DNS & identity binding
  • Cryptographically verifiable DID ownership
  • Zero configuration on your side
  • Future migration path to your own domain

Security & Identity Verification

We only approve requests if we are **confident that you are who you claim to be**.

Accepted Proof Examples

  • Verified Instagram, OnlyFans, X, TikTok, YouTube or similar creator account
  • Your Bluesky profile links to your verified account
  • Your verified account links back to your Bluesky profile
  • Verified website domain ownership

Requests without **strong identity verification will be rejected**. We reserve the right to deny or revoke any handle **without explanation**.

How It Works

1 — You Send Us Your DID

In Bluesky, go to: Settings → Account → Change Handle → I have my own domain. There you will see your **DID** (Decentralized Identifier).

This is the only technical data we need from you. We manage **all DNS records and verification** on our side.

2 — We Configure Everything

We create and manage the required (public) TXT records and bind your DID to your new handle. No DNS access, no technical setup required from your side.

3 — Your Handle Goes Live

Once configured, your new handle becomes active instantly.
