Free early-access Bluesky / ATProto handles under our domains. Professional identity. Verified ownership. Zero cost — while we test and harden our infrastructure.

Free Handle Program

We are currently offering **free custom Bluesky handles** under selected domains we operate. This program exists to **test, validate, and harden our identity infrastructure**.

Early users help us stress-test automation, DNS control, DID resolution and security flows. In return, you receive **a free professional handle**.

What You Get

  • Custom handle under our domains (e.g. @yourname.bapu.app or @yourname.idroot.org )
  • Fully managed DNS & identity binding
  • Cryptographically verifiable DID ownership
  • Zero configuration on your side
  • Future migration path to your own domain

Security & Identity Verification

We only approve requests if we are **confident that you are who you claim to be**. To ensure this, all users must verify their identity through our provider. This process may involve a liveness check and/or an ID check.

Requests without **successful identity verification will be rejected**. We reserve the right to deny or revoke any handle **without explanation**.

How It Works

1 — You Provide Your Profile Link

We extract your **DID** (Decentralized Identifier) automatically from your profile link. You don't need to look up any technical identifiers manually.

2 — Identity Verification

You will be directed to our verification provider to complete an identity check. This may include a liveness check and/or providing a government-issued ID. A handle will only be granted after successful verification.

3 — We Configure Everything

We manage **all DNS records and verification** on our side to link your profile to the new handle. We create and manage the required (public) TXT records and bind your DID to your new handle. No DNS access, no technical setup required from your side.

4 — You Activate Your Handle

Once we have activated the handle on our side, you need to complete the switch in your Bluesky settings:

Settings → Account → Handle → I have my own domain → Verify DNS record

5 — Your Handle Goes Live

Once verified, your new handle becomes active instantly. You can always go back autonomously to your original Bluesky handle at any time through the same settings menu.

Why Free?

This program exists to **test and validate our identity infrastructure under real-world conditions**. Early users help us identify edge cases, operational limits, and automation gaps.

Additionally, seeing professional handles like @name.bapu.app in the wild is great advertising for us. In exchange for this visibility and your feedback, we provide these handles at **zero cost** during this testing phase.
