About BAPU

BAPU is a startup dedicated to evolving the decentralized social web.

We believe that social infrastructure should be open, federated, and secure. Our mission is to integrate powerful encryption in a way that is friendly to the federated nature of the AT Protocol.

Our Vision

We are building tools and infrastructure that enable private, secure communication for:

  • Friends and Family
  • Businesses and Teams
  • Private Groups

By combining the openness of the AT Protocol with zero-trust encryption principles, we empower users to maintain true sovereignty over their digital lives without sacrificing the benefits of a global, connected network.

Startup Spirit

As a young company, we move fast and iterate based on user needs. BAPU is currently in an experimental phase as we harden our infrastructure and refine our encryption models. We are excited to have you with us on this journey toward a more private and resilient social future.
