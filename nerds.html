Because someone always asks in the comments, yes, we take security very seriously.

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

You want military-grade? You got it. We use AES-256 encryption. That means if every computer on Earth tried to crack a single key at the same time, the sun would explode before they succeeded. We think that's secure enough for your private group chats.

📜

NIST Standards

We don't just make things up. We take the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) guidelines into account for our cryptography, key management, and security protocols. So when we say your data is safe, we mean it's "approved by smart people in lab coats" safe.
