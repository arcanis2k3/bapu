01Introduction

Aureus Z, Inc. ("we") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your Personal Data. We own and operate ZChat (bapu.app), a microblogging application on the AT Protocol. Your use of ZChat is subject to this Policy, the Terms of Service, Bapu Community Guidelines, and Copyright Policy. We are a "data controller" of the Personal Data we process in connection with the Services.

02What is Personal Data?

"Personal Data" is information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with a particular person or household. We may also collect deidentified, aggregated, or anonymised data that is not Personal Data.

03Informing Us of Changes

Please keep us informed if your Personal Data changes so we can keep our records accurate. Update your Personal Data in the App or on the Site.

04Applicability of this Policy

This Policy is separate from our Terms of Service and Bapu Community Guidelines, which also govern your use of ZChat.

05Third-Party Services

ZChat may include links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. You should review the privacy policies of those third parties.

06If You Do Not Want to Provide Personal Data

We need to collect certain Personal Data to give you access to ZChat. If you do not want to provide it, we may not be able to provide you with access to ZChat or specific features.

07Updates to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material updates by message in the App, on the Site, or by email. You should review this Privacy Policy every time you access a ZChat service.

08Personal Data We Collect

Account Creation: email address, phone number, profile images, birth date, and username. Your Posts: public — exercise care when deciding what to share. Your Direct Messages: stored unencrypted, accessible for Trust & Safety purposes. Usage Information (automatic): IP address, browser/device info, activity data. Third-Party Age Verification: where required by law. We do not retain biometric data.

09Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to make ZChat available, and analytical cookies to operate and improve ZChat. You can block cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit access to some features. Where required by law, you will be asked to consent to certain cookies.

10How We Use Personal Data

We use Personal Data to: provide and manage your account; answer support requests; communicate policy changes; pursue legitimate interests such as fraud prevention and analytics; send marketing communications (opt-out available via unsubscribe link); and fulfill legal obligations.

11How We Share Your Personal Data

Your posts and public profile are visible to other users by design. We share data with service providers (including Cloudflare for traffic and security), business partners, legal authorities when required, professional advisors, and in the event of a corporate transaction such as a merger or acquisition.

12International Data Transfers

We may transfer personal information internationally. For transfers from the EEA, Switzerland, Brazil, or the UK to countries without adequate protection, we use appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses.

13Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Your Privacy Choices

Email Communications: If you receive a marketing email from us, you may opt out by using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of such email. You will continue to receive transaction-related, non-promotional emails. You will not be opted out of those communications.

Mobile Devices: We may send you push notifications through our App when you enable them. You may opt out by changing the settings on your mobile device. We may collect precise location-based information via the App, but only if you permit us to do so. You may opt out of this collection by changing the settings on your mobile device.

"Do Not Track": We currently do not respond to or honor DNT signals or similar mechanisms transmitted by web browsers, as there is no consistent industry standard for compliance.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on what laws apply to your Personal Data, you may have the right to:

• Request Access to and Portability of Your Personal Information, including obtaining a copy of your personal information and receiving it in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (right of data portability);

• Request Correction of your personal information where it is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated;

• Request Deletion, Anonymization or Blocking of personal information (right to be forgotten);

• Request to Opt-Out of Certain Processing Activities;

• Request Restriction of or Object to our processing of your personal information;

• Withdraw your Consent to our processing — note that withdrawal only affects future processing and does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing;

• Be Informed about third parties with which your personal information has been shared; and

• Request the Review of Decisions Taken Exclusively Based on Automated Processing.

Please contact us using the information in Section 18 to exercise any of these rights. We will process your requests in accordance with applicable laws. Before fulfilling your request, we may ask you to verify your identity. We may retain information for backups, archiving, prevention of fraud and abuse, analytics, or satisfaction of legal obligations. Some laws may allow you to appeal our decision if we decline to process your request.

14Retention of Personal Data

We retain Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Backups are encrypted and retained on a rolling 30-day cycle, after which they are permanently and securely deleted.

When we no longer need your Personal Data for the purposes described in this Policy, we will delete or anonymise it. If deletion is not immediately possible (for example, because your personal information has been stored in backup archives), we will securely store your personal information and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible.

16Security

We make reasonable efforts to protect your information by using physical and electronic safeguards designed to improve the security of the information we maintain. However, as our Services are hosted electronically, we can make no guarantees as to the security or privacy of your information.

If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please notify us immediately by contacting us at contact@bapu.app.

17Children's Personal Information

The ZChat App Services are not directed to children under 13 (or other age as required by local law outside of the United States). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has uploaded personal information to our site without your consent, you may contact us using the information provided in Section 18. If we become aware that a child under 13 (or other applicable minimum age) has provided us with Personal Data without appropriate consent, we will take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.
