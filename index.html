A Space for Your People

BAPU is a social platform built for real relationships. Whether you want a private space for your friends and family or a vibrant hub for your community, BAPU puts you in control.

Why BAPU?

We believe social media should work for you, not the other way around. BAPU provides the tools you need to connect authentically, share securely, and build communities without compromises.

What We Offer

Private Groups

Create secure, invite-only spaces perfect for friends, family, and close circles. Share moments that matter with the people who matter most.

Thriving Communities

Build and grow open or moderated communities around shared interests, hobbies, or local events. Everyone is welcome.

Total Privacy

Your data belongs to you. No tracking, no invasive algorithms, and no selling your personal information. Just genuine connections.
